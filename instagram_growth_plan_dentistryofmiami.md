## 4) Highlights Optimization (Step 2.1)

- Team 🤝🏻 → **Team**
- Funny videos → **Fun**
- Birthdays 🎂 → **Office**
- Education → **Tips**
- Our Services → **Services**

### Final Highlight Order
1. Services
2. Results
3. Reviews
4. Team
5. Location
6. FAQ
7. Tips
8. Office
9. Fun

### How to reorder (Instagram behavior)
Instagram sorts highlights by last update. To move one forward:
1. Edit Highlight
2. Add one temporary story
3. Save
4. Remove temporary story (optional)

Apply updates in reverse order to get final sequence.

## 8) Content Framework (30 Days)

- 40% Education (tips, myths, FAQs)
- 30% Proof (results, testimonials)
- 20% Authority (team, process, clinic standards)
- 10% Conversion (offers, booking reminders)

CTA in every post: **DM “BOOK”**

## 9) KPI Tracking (Weekly)
Track these metrics every 7 days:
- Profile visits
- DMs received
- Booking DMs (keyword BOOK)
- Reach (non-followers)
- Saves
- Shares
- Follows

Decision rules:
- Repeat formats with high saves/shares
- Rewrite hooks on low-retention reels
- Keep one CTA only to reduce friction
